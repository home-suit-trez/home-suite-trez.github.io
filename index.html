How Trézor Suite Works

1

Download Trézor Suite

Download the Trézor Suite desktop app for the best experience, or use the web app to manage your wallet.

2

Install the App

Install the desktop app on your computer. This step is optional if you prefer the web app.

3

Set Up Your Trézor

Connect your Trézor device and follow the guided setup in Trézor Suite. It takes about 15 minutes.

4

Manage Your Crypto Securely

Use Trézor Suite to send, receive, and exchange cryptocurrencies with maximum security.

Why Choose Trézor Suite?

Trézor Suite is the official software for managing your Trézor hardware wallet, designed to provide the highest level of security and ease of use.

  • Open-source and transparent software ensuring trust and security.
  • Supports over 1,000 cryptocurrencies and tokens.
  • Seamless firmware updates and device management.
  • Built-in exchange and portfolio tracking features.
  • Advanced privacy features including Tor integration.
  • Regular security audits and continuous improvements.
